the benefits of stories

Developing the spirit of imagination and creativity in the child: Children have a tremendous ability to imagine, but in light of being surrounded by smart devices and screens surrounding them everywhere, their ability to imagine weakens and they find it difficult to because everything is already in front of them, so storytelling helps to develop Children's sense of imagination and perception of events and characters.

Increase the child's vocabulary: With every line you read to your child, his vocabulary increases and he learns more words, even if you think he is not affected by them. Pronunciation and the development of his vocabulary.

Developing child listening skills: Most children are not good at listening to others, they are busy expressing their requirements and reaching their goals, and listening to stories develops your child's ability to listen in general and increases their attention and ability to focus. Encouraging the child to love reading: the child who grows up listening to stories will only satisfy his passion when he grows up by reading books. You will not need to urge your child to read and talk to him about its importance. young, and he will complete his own path.
